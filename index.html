What is Echo?

Echo is comprised of a website and mobile-application that implements a unique approach to visualizing information. We feel that Virtual Reality is the ultimate medium to immerse someone inside of an audio environment, and it will promote the highest level of understanding in our users. The combination of the website and mobile-application will help teach students and give them the resources they need to learn about audio environments. More specifically, it focuses on sound wave interaction for audio engineering. Our mobile-application gives students and teachers alike the ability to visualize sound waves in an audio environment through the use of our 3D Virtual Reality mobile-application that has been built in Unity. It presents our users the option to be able to select a room they would like to visualize sound within as well as the ability to alter the frequency and amplitude of the sound waves. It also allows users to be able to choose how sound should be displayed within the audio sound environment, whether that be sound waves or dots. Our goal is to lower the barrier of entry into the audio engineering and sound design industry for all of our users. Our website complements our mobile-application by supplying tutorials on how to use the mobile-application, how to create rooms and then visualize them, as well as alternate ways on how to visualize sound in a 2-dimensional audio sound environment.
